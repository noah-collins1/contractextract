SOFTWARE AS A SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Software as a Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2025 ("Effective Date") by and between CloudAnalytics Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Provider"), and Acme Corporation, a California corporation ("Customer").

1. SERVICES

Provider shall provide Customer with access to its cloud-based analytics platform ("Service"), hosted on Provider's infrastructure, available 24 hours per day, 7 days per week.

2. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT

Provider guarantees 99.95% monthly uptime for the Service, calculated monthly, excluding scheduled maintenance windows not to exceed 4 hours per month. Monthly uptime is measured from the first to the last day of each calendar month.

3. SUPPORT

Provider will provide email and phone support with the following response times:
- Priority 1 (Critical): 2-hour response time
- Priority 2 (High): 4-hour response time
- Priority 3 (Medium): 8-hour response time
- Priority 4 (Low): 24-hour response time

Support is available Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM Eastern Time, excluding federal holidays.

4. TERM AND RENEWAL

The initial term of this Agreement is twelve (12) months from the Effective Date. This Agreement will automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month terms unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days prior to the end of the then-current term.

5. TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience by providing sixty (60) days' written notice to the other party. Customer may terminate immediately for cause if Provider fails to meet the uptime commitment for three (3) consecutive months.

6. CUSTOMER DATA

Customer retains all right, title, and interest in and to all Customer Data. Upon termination of this Agreement, Provider will retain Customer Data for thirty (30) days to allow Customer to retrieve it, after which Provider will permanently delete all Customer Data from its systems.

7. DATA PORTABILITY

Customer may export Customer Data at any time during the term of this Agreement in standard formats (CSV, JSON, XML). Provider will assist Customer with data export upon request at no additional charge.

8. SECURITY AND COMPLIANCE

Provider maintains SOC 2 Type II certification and complies with ISO 27001 standards. Provider implements industry-standard encryption for data at rest and in transit. Provider will notify Customer of any security breaches affecting Customer Data within seventy-two (72) hours of discovery.

9. FEES AND PAYMENT

Customer shall pay annual subscription fees of $120,000 USD in advance within thirty (30) days of invoice date. Fees are non-refundable except as provided in Section 10 (Service Level Credits).

10. SERVICE LEVEL CREDITS

If Provider fails to meet the 99.95% uptime commitment in any month, Customer shall be entitled to service level credits calculated as follows:
- 99.0% - 99.94% uptime: 5% credit
- 98.0% - 98.99% uptime: 10% credit
- Below 98.0% uptime: 25% credit

Credits are Customer's sole and exclusive remedy for Provider's failure to meet the uptime commitment.

11. LIABILITY CAP

Provider's total aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the fees paid by Customer in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim, except for liability arising from Provider's gross negligence, willful misconduct, or fraud.

12. FRAUD EXCEPTION

Notwithstanding any other provision in this Agreement, Provider's liability for fraud or willful misconduct shall be unlimited and not subject to the liability cap in Section 11.

13. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to its conflict of laws provisions. Any disputes shall be resolved in the state or federal courts located in Wilmington, Delaware.

14. CONFIDENTIALITY

Each party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of the other party's Confidential Information for a period of three (3) years after the termination of this Agreement.
